Subscriber guide — two minutes to get started

Lesson Forge Pro sends you weekly intelligence briefings and living courses in the fields you follow — content that updates itself with what's actually happening in the market, in English and Arabic.

1 · Create your account

  1. Open /en/subscribe (Arabic: /ar/subscribe), enter your name, email, and a password.
  2. Sign in. You start on the free tier: previews of every field's weekly briefings.

2 · Your two pages

PageWhat you'll find
BriefingsThe weekly feed per field: what moved, why it matters, what to learn next. Free tier shows the opening; full subscribers read everything.
Course libraryStructured courses built from those fields. Open a course, read each session's plan, and tick sessions off as you complete them — your progress bar follows you.

3 · Free vs full access

FreeFull subscription (per field)
Weekly briefingsOpening previewComplete briefing + full email digest
CoursesSession plansFull session guides
Downloads—Slides (PPTX), handouts (PDF/Word), assessments — including Arabic editions

To upgrade a field to full access, contact us — activation is immediate.

4 · Weekly digest email

Every week, the briefing for each field you follow lands in your inbox — the complete briefing on full subscriptions, a teaser on the free tier. No action needed; it follows your subscriptions automatically.

5 · Arabic

The whole experience works in Arabic: use the عربية link in the sidebar (or visit any page under /ar). Full-access downloads include culturally adapted Arabic editions, laid out right-to-left correctly.

Questions or upgrade requests: reply to any digest email or contact the address on your invoice.
