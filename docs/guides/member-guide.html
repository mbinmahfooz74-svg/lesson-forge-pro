Lesson Forge Pro

Workspace member guide — one page

Your company has a private AI training engine. As a member, you use it to build courses and generate teaching material carrying your company's branding — in English and Arabic.

1 · Join

  1. Your workspace admin sends an invitation email. Open the link (valid 7 days), enter your name and a password.
  2. Sign in at /en/login — or /ar/login for the Arabic interface.

2 · What each page does

PageUse it to
VerticalsSee your company's training fields — topic maps, proposed courses, EN/AR glossary. Create a new field with a one-sentence description.
Source pipelinePaste any URL — article, YouTube video, PDF — to feed the engine. Better sources → better material.
CoursesPlan a course from a topic; per session, Draft the educator guide and Generate materials — branded slides, assessment with rubric, learner handouts (PDF/Word).
Review queueThe engine's weekly proposals wait here. Approve what's worth teaching (approval auto-plans the course); reject the noise.
FeedbackAfter teaching, paste your transcript or debrief notes — the engine learns your style and drafts better next time.

3 · A typical task, end to end

  1. Courses → type "Fire safety essentials for site supervisors" → Plan course.
  2. Open session 1 → Draft → read the minute-by-minute guide.
  3. Generate materials → download the branded slides and handouts.
  4. Teach it → paste your debrief into Feedback. Done.
Need a seat for a colleague, a brand change, or something looks wrong? Talk to your workspace admin — they manage members and can escalate to the platform operator.
